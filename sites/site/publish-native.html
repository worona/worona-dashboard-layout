1. Create your app name and icon

Create a name and icon for your mobile app and let users easily identify it amongst other apps.

We recommend keeping your app name to around 12 characters or less. Long names may be truncated, which means users will not see all the characters on their phones or tablets.

This shall be a square image with at least 1024x1024px resolution.
Try using your logo, or a unique shape or symbol to make your icon easily recognizable.

3. Publish

Here you can choose whether you want to publish your app by yourself, or if you prefer us to do the work for you.

Do it yourself

Before starting, please note that you will need to:
  • Compile your apps using Phonegap Build.
  • Have a Google developer account or create one, which costs 35$.
  • Have a Apple developer account or create one, which costs 99$/yr.
  • Create the screenshots and fill all the required submission forms.
  • Upload your apps to the Google Play and Apple App Store (you need a Mac computer to upload it to the App Store).
Whenever you're ready, download our .zip file below and check out our Help Documentation, it will guide you through the next steps.
