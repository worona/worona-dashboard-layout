1. Create your app name and icon

Create a name and icon for your mobile app and let users easily identify it amongst other apps.

We recommend keeping your app name to around 12 characters or less. Long names may be truncated, which means users will not see all the characters on their phones or tablets.

This shall be a square image with at least 1024x1024px resolution.
Try using your logo, or a unique shape or symbol to make your icon easily recognizable.

Frequently asked questions about the publish to the stores

There is no way to publish the Apps to the App Stores for free?
Unfortunately not. Apple and Google, owners of the Android & iOS App Stores require developer accounts to submit the Apps.

The price of these developer Accounts is:

• 35$ Google Developer Account
• 99$/year Apple Developer Account

In addition, you need a Mac computer to upload the iOS App to the Stores.

What is included in the Worona Publish service?
Everything. With our hassle-free service you don’t need to compile your app or have any developer account.

We’ll go through the whole submission process for you and upload your apps in Worona’s account, so you don’t have to pay Google or Apple to have your own account.

The price of the service includes an iOS and and Android App. With no extra fees.

Your App will be published in 7 working days.

Choose the most convinient way to have your App published Worona Publish.

Do I need to be a developer to use the Do It yourself?
No, but you will definitely be able to deal with a complicate process.

We have worked hard to make Worona an easy platform ready to be used by anyone. However, the submission to the stores involves some technical processes that can be complex.

We encourage everybody to use Phonegap Build, in addition there are lots of guides and tutorials on how to submit an .apk and an .ipa to the stores.

But keep in mind that you will also need Developer Accounts that cost more than 130$ combined.

Is Phonegap Build free?
Yes. You can take advantage of Phonegap Build free plan to compile your app and get a .apk and a .ipa

Afterwards you will need Google and Apple Developer Accounts to publish your App to the stores.

If you choose Worona Publish you don't have to worry about the Phonegap process or the Developer Accounts.
